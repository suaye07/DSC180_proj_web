Designed to empower users to buy, sell, and utilize data securely and in compliance with privacy laws. Built on the Ethereum blockchain with IPFS decentralized storage, our dApp ensures a seamless and transparent experience for both data providers and buyers.

Features

Explore the unique functionalities of our platform that redefine data exchange.

Smart Contracts

Ensuring trust and security in every transaction using Ethereum-based smart contracts.

Secure Payments

Buy and sell datasets securely through smart contracts and encrypted transactions.

Data Encryption & Security

Data is encrypted, verified for malware, and certified for originality.

PII Scrubbing & Validation

Our system detects and removes personal information (PII) using advanced AI validation and AWS Lambda.

Decentralized Storage

How It Works
