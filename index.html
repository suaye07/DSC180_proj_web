Welcome to the Decentralized Data Marketplace

Designed to empower users to buy, sell, and utilize data securely and in compliance with privacy laws. Built on the Ethereum blockchain and leveraging the Polygon network for efficient transactions, our dapp ensures a seamless and transparent experience for both data providers and buyers.

How It Works

Create an account and set up a wallet for transactions.

Step 2: Upload Data

Encrypt, anonymize, and certify data for secure storage.

Step 3: List on Marketplace

Tag datasets with descriptions and industry tags for discovery.

Step 4: Filter and Buy

Step 5: Analytics

Exchange analytics services or use in-house ML tools for insights.

Marketplace

Browse, buy, and sell data seamlessly in our secure platform.

The marketplace is where data providers and buyers connect to exchange datasets securely. Providers upload certified data tagged by industry, description, and column details, ensuring easy navigation for buyers.

Buyers can preview datasets, purchase with platform tokens, and utilize advanced analytics services offered by the platform or other users.
